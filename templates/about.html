Our History

Established in 1950, GOVT. BOYS HIGH SCHOOL PETH MAKHAMA has been a cornerstone of education in the region for over 70 years.

We began as a small village school and have grown into one of the most respected government schools in Jammu and Kashmir. Our alumni include prominent civil servants, doctors, engineers, and leaders who have made significant contributions to society.

Our school in the early days

Our Mission & Vision
Mission:

To provide quality education that empowers students from all backgrounds to achieve their full potential and become responsible citizens.

Vision:

To be a center of excellence that nurtures intellectual curiosity, creativity, and character development.

We strive to:
Deliver excellent academic instruction
Foster critical thinking and creativity
Promote values of integrity and service
Develop well-rounded individuals
